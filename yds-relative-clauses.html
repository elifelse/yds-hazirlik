Relative Clauses

Sıfat cümleleri YDS gramer bölümünün vazgeçilmezi. Who, which, that, whose, where, when — hepsini örneklerle, tuzaklarıyla öğren.

📖 Temel Relative Pronouns

ZamirNe için kullanılırÖrnek
whoİnsanlar için (özne veya nesne)The scientist who discovered penicillin was Fleming.
whomİnsanlar için (nesne, formal)The professor whom I met was inspiring.
whichNesneler ve hayvanlar içinThe book which I bought is fascinating.
thatİnsanlar veya nesneler (defining only)The report that was published last year...
whoseİyelik (kimin, neyin)The researcher whose paper won the award...
whereYerThe laboratory where they work is modern.
whenZamanThe era when computers emerged...
whySebep (the reason why)The reason why he failed is unclear.

✂️ Defining vs Non-Defining

Defining (Tanımlayıcı) — Virgülsüz

Cümlenin anlamı için zorunlu bilgi verir. Çıkarırsan anlam değişir.

The students who study regularly get higher scores.
"who study regularly" olmadan hangi öğrencilerden bahsedildiği belli olmaz — bu yüzden tanımlayıcı.
Kural: Defining cümlelerde that kullanılabilir. which ve who ile de olur. Virgül YOK.

Non-Defining (Tanımlayıcı Olmayan) — Virgüllü

Ek bilgi verir, parantez gibi. Çıkarılabilir, anlam değişmez.

Einstein, who was born in Germany, developed the theory of relativity.
Einstein zaten belirli biri. "who was born in Germany" ek bilgi — virgülle ayrılır.
Kritik kural: Non-defining cümlelerde that KULLANILAMAZ. Sadece who / which / whose kullanılır.

🧠 Whose — En Çok Karıştırılan

whose her zaman bir ismin önünde gelir ve o ismin sahibini belirtir.

The company whose profits declined filed for bankruptcy.
whose + profits = şirketin kârları
Scientists whose research focuses on climate change...
whose + research = bilim insanlarının araştırması
✗ The book who's cover is torn... (YANLIŞ — who's = who is)
✓ The book whose cover is torn... (DOĞRU)

⚠️ YDS'de En Çok Çıkan Tuzaklar

❗ Tuzak 1: Non-defining'de "that" kullanmak

Virgüllü cümlelerde that kullanılamaz. YDS'de sık çıkar.

✗ The Eiffel Tower, that was built in 1889, stands 330m.
✓ The Eiffel Tower, which was built in 1889, stands 330m.

❗ Tuzak 2: Nesne pozisyonunda zamir tekrarı

Relative clause içinde nesneyi tekrar etme — zamir zaten o görevi üstleniyor.

✗ The report which I read it yesterday was useful.
✓ The report which I read yesterday was useful.

❗ Tuzak 3: where/in which karışıklığı

where = in which / at which. İkisi de doğru ama soru hangisinin cümleye uyduğunu sorar.

✓ The city where she grew up is beautiful.
✓ The city in which she grew up is beautiful.
✗ The city which she grew up is beautiful. (in/at eksik)

❗ Tuzak 4: Preposition + which sırası

Formal yazıda preposition relative pronoun'un önüne geçer. YDS akademik metinlerde bu yapı çok çıkar.

✓ The method by which data was collected is reliable. (formal)
✓ The method which data was collected by is reliable. (informal)

🔧 Reduced Relative Clauses (Kısaltılmış)

YDS okuma ve cümle tamamlama bölümünde sık karşılaşılan yapı:

Tam haliKısaltılmış haliNe zaman?
...who is working on the project...working on the projectAktif, devam eden eylem
...which was published in 2020...published in 2020Pasif, tamamlanmış eylem
...who have been selected...selected for the programPasif perfect
...which contains data...containing dataAktif, özellik bildirme
Kural: Aktif ise -ing, pasif ise -ed / past participle kullan. Bu yapılar özellikle okuma parçalarında anlamı doğru kavramak için kritik.

✏️ Pratik Sorular

1. The experiment ___ results surprised everyone was conducted last year.
(whose / which / that / who)

Cevap: whose — "results" bir isim, önünde iyelik ilişkisi var → whose results

2. Darwin, ___ theory of evolution changed science, was born in 1809.
(that / who / whose / which)

Cevap: whose — Non-defining (virgüllü) + iyelik: whose theory. "that" kullanamazsın.

3. The university ___ she studied is ranked among the top 100 worldwide.
(where / which / in that / that)

Cevap: where — "university" bir yer. "at which" de doğru olurdu ama seçeneklerde yok. "that" kullanmak için "that she studied at" gerekir.

4. The paper ___ by the research team contained groundbreaking findings.
(written / writing / wrote / write)

Cevap: written — Reduced relative clause, pasif: "which was written" → "written"
