YDS'de
Passive Voice

Edilgen yapı YDS'nin hem gramer hem de anlam yakınlığı bölümlerinde sık çıkar. Tüm zamanlar, by-phrase ve sınav tuzakları burada.

📐 Temel Formül

be (doğru zamanda)  +  past participle (V3)

Passive yapı kurmak için önce özneyi nesneye dönüştür, fiilin "be" kısmını zamanına göre çekimle, asıl fiili V3 (past participle) yapıp ekle.

Active: Scientists discovered penicillin in 1928.
Passive: Penicillin was discovered by scientists in 1928.
Özne ("Scientists") → "by + noun" oldu. Nesne ("penicillin") → yeni özne. "discover" → V3 "discovered".

📅 Tüm Zamanlarda Passive

ZamanActivePassive
Simple PresentThey test the samples.The samples are tested.
Simple PastThey tested the samples.The samples were tested.
Present PerfectThey have tested the samples.The samples have been tested.
Past PerfectThey had tested the samples.The samples had been tested.
Future (will)They will test the samples.The samples will be tested.
Present ContinuousThey are testing the samples.The samples are being tested.
Past ContinuousThey were testing the samples.The samples were being tested.
Modal (can/must/should)They should test the samples.The samples should be tested.
YDS'de en çok: Simple past passive, present perfect passive ve modal passive çıkar. "have been + V3" yapısını iyi tanı.

🔗 By-Phrase Kullanımı

Edilgen cümlede eylemi yapan kişi/şey önemliyse by + özne eklenir. Önemsizse (ya da bilinmiyorsa) by kullanılmaz.

The theory was proposed by Einstein in 1905.
Yapan kişi önemli → "by Einstein" var.
The building was demolished (by workers) last year.
"By workers" gereksiz — kimin yıktığı önemli değil, sınavda genellikle eklenmez.

⚠️ By vs. With

  • by → eylemi yapan kişi/araç: The letter was written by her.
  • with → kullanılan nesne/alet: The letter was written with a pen.

💬 "It is said / believed / reported" Yapısı

YDS'de anlam yakınlığı sorularında bu impersonal passive yapısı çok çıkar. İki farklı forma dönüşebilir:

Form 1: It is said that the climate is changing.
Form 2: The climate is said to be changing.
It is... thatSubject + is/are ... to
It is believed that exercise improves mood.Exercise is believed to improve mood.
It is reported that prices have risen.Prices are reported to have risen.
It is expected that the team will win.The team is expected to win.
It is known that water boils at 100°C.Water is known to boil at 100°C.
İpucu: "that" cümlesinde perfect tense varsa (has/have + V3) → infinitive'de to have + V3 kullan: "is said to have discovered".

🔨 Passive + Modal Kombinasyonları

Gramer bölümünde modal + passive soruları çok çıkar. Formül: modal + be + V3

Türkçe AnlamModal PassiveÖrnek
Yapılabilircan be + V3This problem can be solved easily.
Yapılmalıshould be + V3All data should be backed up daily.
Yapılmak zorundamust be + V3The form must be submitted before Friday.
Yapılabilirdi (geçmiş)could have been + V3The accident could have been avoided.
Yapılmış olmalımust have been + V3The keys must have been lost yesterday.

🧪 YDS Örnek Soruları

The new laboratory equipment ______ before the study could begin.

A) has installed   B) had been installed   C) was installing   D) installed

Cevap: B — "Before the study could begin" → ekipman daha önce tamamlanmış olmalı = past perfect passive: had been installed. A gramerce yanlış (has + V3 → geçmişte had been olmalı). C aktif continuous. D aktif.

It is widely accepted that exercise ______ mood and cognitive function.

A) has improved   B) is improving   C) improves   D) is improved

Cevap: C — "It is accepted that..." yapısında ikinci cümle aktif: "exercise improves". D yanlış çünkü "exercise" yapan, edilgen alamaz bu bağlamda.

The ancient ruins ______ to have been built by a civilization that disappeared 3,000 years ago.

A) are believed   B) believe   C) believed   D) are believing

Cevap: A — "Subject + are believed to have been..." = impersonal passive yapısı. B aktif (özne kim?). C past tense. D yanlış (believe progressive kullanılmaz).

Considering the severity of the situation, drastic measures ______ immediately.

A) must take   B) must be taken   C) must have taken   D) must be taking

Cevap: B — "Measures" eylem yapan değil, eylem yapılan → passive gerekli. must be taken. A aktif (measures take?). C "taken" = geçmişte alınmış, anlam değişiyor. D continuous passive olur ama bağlamla uymuyor.

⚠️ Sık Yapılan Hatalar

⚠️ Stative Fiiller Passive Almaz

Have (sahip olmak), resemble, consist of, belong to, contain gibi stative fiiller passive yapılamaz.
✗ A solution is contained in the bottle. → ✓ The bottle contains a solution.

⚠️ Double Object Passive

Give, send, offer, show gibi iki nesne alan fiillerde her iki nesne de özne olabilir:
Active: They gave the students certificates.
Passive 1: The students were given certificates.
Passive 2: Certificates were given to the students.

⚠️ Get-Passive

Günlük İngilizcede "get + V3" informal passive olarak kullanılır ama YDS akademik metinlerinde bu yapı seçeneklerde genellikle tuzak olarak sunulur.
✗ The report got published last week.
✓ The report was published last week.
